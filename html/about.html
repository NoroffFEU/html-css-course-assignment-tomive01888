About us

We started as an idea as a friend group, consisting of developers, gamers and competitive people. We launched The Arena back in 2015 with the aim of online retail sales of games, equipment and merch.

Our competition

We believe the competition in a game, is just as important on the market prices for video games. As much as we strive to beat our competitors, we also hold once a year Events for you, our customers, as a way to give back to the community in different way.
